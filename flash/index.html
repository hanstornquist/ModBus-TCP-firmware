Modbus TCP Gateway

Flash your gateway

Install the latest firmware (v{{VERSION}}) straight from your browser — no tools to download.

Your browser can’t flash over USB. Use Chrome or Edge on a desktop (Web Serial isn’t available on Firefox, Safari, iOS, or Android). This page must be served over https to access the serial port.

Connect the board over USB, then click Connect.

Steps

  1. Plug the board into your computer with a USB‑C cable.
  2. Put it in download mode: hold the BOOT button, tap RST (or plug in the cable while holding BOOT), then release BOOT. New boards usually start in download mode already.
  3. Click Connect above and pick the board’s serial port.
  4. Choose Install and wait for the flash to finish (~30–60 s). The board reboots itself.

Flashing erases the whole chip, so any saved Wi-Fi settings are cleared — the board returns to a clean factory state.

After flashing

  1. The gateway starts a setup Wi-Fi network named ModBus-TCP-Gateway.
  2. Its unique password is printed on the device label (and on the serial console at boot).
  3. Join that network from a phone/laptop, open the portal, and enter your Wi-Fi details.

Once on your network the gateway updates itself automatically over the air. See the firmware repository for release notes.
